A1 Homework Session 14

Topic: Travel and Last Weekend

Reading

Last weekend Maria went to the beach with her family. They traveled by car. They ate seafood and swam in the ocean. Maria bought a souvenir for her friend.

1. Where did Maria go?

a) To the beach

b) To school

2. How did they travel?

a) By airplane

b) By car

3. What did Maria buy?

a) A souvenir

b) A book

Writing

Write 4–6 sentences about your last weekend.

Speaking

Speak for 20 seconds and describe your last weekend.

You can say:

  • Where you went
  • Who you went with
  • What you did

Grammar

Choose the correct past form.

1. I ___ to the park yesterday.

a) go

b) went

2. She ___ seafood.

a) eat

b) ate

3. They ___ a taxi.

a) take

b) took
